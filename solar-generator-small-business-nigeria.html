Why Petrol Generators Are Bad for Business

Petrol generators have three major problems for Nigerian businesses. First, the noise drives customers away — nobody wants to eat, shop, or get a haircut next to a roaring generator. Second, the fumes are a health hazard for you and your customers inside enclosed spaces. Third, the cost is unpredictable — petrol prices in Nigeria fluctuate constantly, making it impossible to budget your power costs accurately.
